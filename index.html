Welcome,

my name is Xiao Wang. I'm currently pursuing my Ph.D. in the Fudan University NLP group, specializing in large language models. My research journey has been enriched with guidance from Prof. Qi Zhang and Prof. Xuanjing Huang. My past research has largely been centered around information extraction and the robustness of neural networks. Additionally, I've taken the lead in developing and maintaining Textflint,a unified multilingual robustness evaluation toolkit. Recently, I've been focusing on the continuous learning of large models. I believe lifelong learning is essential for advancing intelligence and can significantly reduce LLM deployment costs. Another topic I'm earnestly exploring is responsible AI. With the growing power of LLMs, addressing challenges such as preventing misuse for activities like plagiarism or cheating becomes essential. If you share similar interests or have insights, let's move AI forward, together!
